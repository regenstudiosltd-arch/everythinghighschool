Project Goal

Use the uploaded website as a reference only for understanding the business and content.

Do NOT copy the design.

Create a completely new, premium, modern, and highly polished UI/UX that is significantly better in aesthetics, usability, hierarchy, and customer experience.

The website should feel like a combination of Apple, Stripe, Amazon, Airbnb, and Jumia Ghana, while remaining clean, educational, and trustworthy.

The design should be suitable for both parents and students.
