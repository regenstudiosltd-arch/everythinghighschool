Update the website design with the following changes. These changes replace the previous "Package" terminology and improve the shopping flow.

# 9. User Experience

The entire process should feel like a guided shopping assistant:

1. Choose Boarding or Day Prospectus.
2. View the official GES Harmonized Prospectus.
3. Add required items or buy everything.
4. (Optional) Visit the TVET section to add department-specific tools.
5. Choose a ChopBox.
6. Customize the contents.
7. Select a Metallic Chop Box, Plastic Storage Box, or use an existing one.
8. Review the order.
9. Pay in full or use **Pay Small Small**.
10. Track the order, payments, and delivery from the customer dashboard.

The design should be clean, intuitive, and reassuring, helping parents and students complete all their SHS shopping in one seamless experience.
