# MASTER PROMPT FOR LOVABLE

## Project Overview

Build a **production-ready, enterprise-grade, responsive web application** called **Aneres Everything High School (AES)**.

This is **NOT just an ecommerce website.**

It is Ghana's **first complete digital platform for Senior High School admissions, prospectus shopping, examination vouchers, school supplies, chop box packages, payment plans and educational services.**

The platform should feel like a combination of:

* Apple (clean UI)
* Stripe (beautiful layouts)
* Amazon (shopping experience)
* Jumia Ghana (local ecommerce)
* Airbnb (easy onboarding)
* Notion (clean dashboard)

The website must be significantly better than the current version in every aspect.

# USER EXPERIENCE GOAL

The website should make parents feel confident and supported through every stage of preparing a student for Senior High School. Instead of navigating multiple shops and services, they should be able to discover schools, purchase prospectuses, buy exam vouchers, customize chop box packages, shop for all required supplies, choose flexible payment plans, and track every order from one intuitive platform. The experience should be fast, trustworthy, and enjoyable on both mobile and desktop.

---

# FINAL INSTRUCTION TO LOVABLE

Do not build a simple business website. Build a **scalable digital education commerce platform** with clean architecture, reusable components, responsive design, enterprise-level security, and room for future expansion. Prioritize an intuitive user experience, fast performance, and maintainable code. The platform should be visually polished enough to compete with leading e-commerce websites while remaining focused on the needs of Ghanaian parents and students preparing for Senior High School.
